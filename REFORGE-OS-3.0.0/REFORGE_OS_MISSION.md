## Mission

**REFORGE OS** is focused on being the **best repair shop software** for all repair shops.

---

## Core Purpose

### What REFORGE OS Does
✅ **Diagnostics** for all devices (Android, iOS, Windows, Linux, Mac, phones, computers, tablets)  
✅ **Find Solutions** and fix problems  
✅ **Deploy Fixes** through guided workflows  
✅ **Boot Recovery** for crashed/unbootable PCs and Macs  
✅ **Device Detection** via BootForge USB (all devices: Apple, Android, all)  
✅ **Case Management** for repair shops  
✅ **Compliance & Audit** logging

### What REFORGE OS Does NOT Do
❌ Secret Rooms (Sonic Codex, Ghost Codex, Pandora Codex)  
❌ Forensic toolkits  
❌ Codex systems  

*These features stay in the Bobbys-Workshop--3.0.0 folder*
