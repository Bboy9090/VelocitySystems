## WHY STRESS BEATS BENCHMARKS

### Benchmarks are polite. Stress tests are honest.

**The Problem with Benchmarks:**

- Short-duration bursts hide thermal issues
- Synthetic workloads don't mirror real use
- GPU/CPU boost masks instability
- Storage tests miss real-world patterns

**What Stress Tests Reveal:**

- **Thermal collapse** after 20-30 minutes
- **Power delivery instability** under sustained load
- **Memory pressure** from real applications
- **Driver conflicts** under professional workloads

**Example:**

A Mac that "works" but fails Final Cut Pro is not healthy.  
A phone that passes benchmarks but drops calls under load is not repaired.

**We test what you actually use.**
