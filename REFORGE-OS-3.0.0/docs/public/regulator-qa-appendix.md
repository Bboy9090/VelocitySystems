# Regulator Q&A Appendix (Verbatim-Ready)

## Q: Do you provide or integrate jailbreaking, rooting, or bypass tools?

**A:** No. The platform performs analysis, classification, documentation, and routing only. We do not execute, distribute, or endorse third-party modification software.

## Q: Why reference third-party research at all?

**A:** Awareness informs risk assessment and appropriate legal routing. No tools, instructions, or links are provided. References are used solely for risk classification and language shaping.

## Q: Can users change devices using your software?

**A:** No. There are no execution paths, command interfaces, or automation. The platform analyzes, classifies, documents, and routes to external authorities.

## Q: How do you prevent misuse?

**A:** Ownership confidence gates, jurisdiction checks, role-based access, immutable audit logs, and external authorization routing. All activity is logged and attributable.

## Q: What data do you collect?

**A:** Non-invasive metadata required for analysis. No credentials or device contents are collected. Audit logs contain action labels and timestamps only.

## Q: What is "Interpretive Review" or "Custodian Vault"?

**A:** An advanced analysis environment for complex legal scenarios. It classifies risk, maps precedent, and identifies required external authorization. It performs no actions and provides no procedural guidance.

## Q: Why approve this app?

**A:** It reduces illegal activity by redirecting users to lawful pathways and stopping prohibited scenarios. It replaces undocumented modification with ownership verification, risk disclosure, and authority routing.
