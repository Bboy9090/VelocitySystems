# Regulator Q&A Appendix (Verbatim-Ready)

## Q: Do you provide or integrate jailbreaking, rooting, or bypass tools?
**A:** No. The platform performs analysis, classification, documentation, and routing only.

## Q: Why reference third-party research at all?
**A:** Awareness informs risk assessment and appropriate legal routing. No tools, instructions, or links are provided.

## Q: Can users change devices using your software?
**A:** No. There are no execution paths, command interfaces, or automation.

## Q: How do you prevent misuse?
**A:** Ownership confidence gates, jurisdiction checks, role-based access, immutable audit logs, and external authorization routing.

## Q: What data do you collect?
**A:** Non-invasive metadata required for analysis. No credentials or device contents.

## Q: What is "Interpretive Review"?
**A:** A gated analytical environment that provides historical and legal context for complex cases. It does not execute actions.

## Q: How do you handle high-risk scenarios?
**A:** The platform classifies risk, requires external authorization, and routes users to appropriate authorities (OEM, carrier, legal).

## Q: Do you store device data?
**A:** No device contents or credentials are collected. Only metadata required for analysis.

## Q: How is auditability enforced?
**A:** All actions are hash-chained, append-only, and immutable. No deletions or modifications are possible.

## Q: Why approve this platform?
**A:** It reduces illegal activity by redirecting users to lawful pathways and stopping prohibited scenarios.
